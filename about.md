# About

I am an engineer and sustainability professional based in India.

Over time, I became increasingly interested in a broader question:

How can societies help people discover meaningful work, build useful skills, and contribute productively to their communities?

That question led me toward education, employment, public policy, urban development, and human capital.

This website documents my attempts to understand these topics through reading, writing, research, and experimentation.

My long-term goal is to help build practical systems and institutions that enable more people to realise their potential and improve their quality of life.

I am currently studying the relationship between education, employment, human development, and national progress.
